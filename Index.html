Bikes

BMW S1000RR

BMW S1000RR is a race oriented sport bike initially made by BMW Motorrad to compete in the 2009 Superbike World Championship, that is now in commercial production. It was introduced in Munich in April 2008, and is powered by a 999 cc (61.0 cu in) four-cylinder engine redlined at 14,200 rpm

Ninja H2R

The Kawasaki Ninja H2R gets its power from a 998cc, inline four-cylinder engine mated to a six-speed gearbox. The supercharged engine is good for producing a maximum power output of 305 hp along with a peak torque of 165 Nm. Thanks to this setup, the motorcycle hits a top speed of 400 kmph.

Benelli TNT 600i

The TNT 600i has an engine displacement of 600 cc with a liquid cooling system. The bike has a four-valve, four-stroke, and four-cylinder engine that offers a 6-speed gearbox. The petrol engine generates a power of 84.00 bhp @ 11500 rpm and a maximum torque of 54.6 Nm @ 10500 rpm.
